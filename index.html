Sajal Debnath

Researcher & Software Engineer

I am a researcher specializing in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Healthcare Analytics, with a particular focus on streaming data analysis and fuzzy systems. I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Barishal in 2022.

My research spans multiple domains, including streaming data analysis and classification, artificial intelligence applications in healthcare, and quantum DNA computing. I have published my work in esteemed journals and conferences such as Applied Intelligence, Healthcare Analytics, the Journal of Intelligent & Fuzzy Systems, and ICAEEE. I completed my Bachelor's thesis under the supervision of Dr. Md Manjur Ahmed. Additionally, I collaborated with Dr. Rahat Hossain Faisal on a project focused on optimizing cancer chemotherapy scheduling. My research experience also extends to interdisciplinary projects in quantum-DNA and DNA-quantum, conducted in partnership with researchers from various institutions and guided by Prof. Dr. Hafiz Md. Hasan Babu.

My research methodologies include adaptive learning algorithms, fuzzy systems, multi-objective optimization, with applications in healthcare diagnostics and treatment planning. I am particularly interested in developing clinically relevant solutions that bridge the gap between theoretical computer science and practical medical applications.

I am open to research collaboration or full time opportunities.
